How Mana and Types work

Mana

  • Mana is your resource (0 to 6). You spend it on bigger actions.
  • Attack always gives +1 Mana and deals damage matching your hero’s primary type.
  • Guard gives +1 Mana and makes the next hit deal 50% less.
  • Magic attacks cost 2 Mana. Fire costs 3 Mana.
  • Heal costs 1 Mana (or 2 if you are Bound). It restores a chunk of HP and grants a small Barrier (next hit −30%). You have limited charges.
  • If you are Bound (Touch effect), your magic costs +1 Mana until it wears off. Guard breaks Bind.

Types

Damage is calculated like this:

Damage = Base × STAB × Effectiveness (rounded)

  • Move type: each action is a type (Wind / Water / Sound / Smell/Taste / Fire / Sight / Earth / Touch).
  • Type-locked attacks: you can only use Attack + spells that match your hero’s types. (Attack always matches your primary type.) Guard and Heal are universal.
  • STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus): if the move matches one of your types, it gets ×1.2.
  • Effectiveness: some types hit harder or softer into other types.
  • Dual types multiply together (example: Wind vs Fire+Sight uses both matchups).
